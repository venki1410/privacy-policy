TVK SPS – Safety Standards Against CSAE

Zero Tolerance Policy

TVK SPS maintains a strict zero-tolerance policy against Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE). We are committed to ensuring our platform is safe for all users and does not host, promote, or distribute any harmful content related to CSAE.

Content Monitoring & Moderation

We regularly monitor user-submitted content using automated tools and manual reviews to detect and remove any content that violates our safety policies. Any detected CSAE content is immediately removed and reported to relevant authorities.

User Reporting

Users are encouraged to report any suspicious or abusive content through the in-app reporting feature or by contacting our support team. All reports are treated with high priority and confidentiality.

Collaboration with Authorities

We cooperate fully with law enforcement agencies and child protection organizations to report and act on any CSAE-related activity detected through our platform.

Safety by Design

Our app is built with safety in mind. We limit user interactions, apply strict content rules, and use secure backend systems to prevent unauthorized access and misuse of the platform.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our safety policies, please contact us at: support@tvksps.com
